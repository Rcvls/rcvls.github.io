What is RaceValues?

RaceValues is, in essence, a quiz that attempts to assign percentages for twelve different race related values. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be compared to the maximum possible for each value, thus giving you a percentage. Answer honestly!

There are questions in the test.

What are the twelve values?

There are six independent axes - Bigotry, Differentialism, Assimilationism, Pluralism, Politicization and Particularism - and each has two opposing values assigned to them. They are:

AGGRESSION

Those with higher Aggression scores strongly dislike, if not hate, people of other races. To them, racial concerns are not just political; they are personal.

BIGOTRY

COURTESY

Those with higher Courtesy scores believe in treating individuals well regardless of their race. They may support race-based policies, but will treat their neighbours with respect no matter their ancestry.

SPECIATION

Those with higher Speciation scores believe that race has serious anatomical or mental consequences beyond just appearance.

DIFFERENTIALISM

UNIFORMITY

Those with higher Uniformity scores believe that race is mostly about appearance and that it has little to no effect on an individual's behaviour or anatomy.

INTEGRATION

Those with higher Integration scores believe that populations can assimilate racially different populations without experiencing significant racial changes.

ASSIMILATIONISM

SEGREGATION

Those with higher Segregation scores believe that race is deeply genetic and that assimilation of racially different elements irrevocably changes the racial characteristics of a population.

DIVERSITY

Those with higher Diversity scores believe that a racially diverse world is desirable. They are likely to support ethnopluralism.

PLURALISM

SUPREMACY

Those with higher Supremacy scores believe that their own race is superior to other races. They see no inherent value in other races staying vital, homogenous and populous.

TOTALITARIAN

Those with higher Totalitarian scores want policies to be implemented to further their ideals about race.

POLITICIZATION

LIBERTARIAN

Those with higher Libertarian scores do not want the state to make policies based on race or with race in mind.

PARTICULAR

Those with higher Particular scores believe view individuals of other races as having lower moral worth than those of their own race.

PARTICULARISM

UNIVERSAL

Those with higher Universal scores believe that moral laws apply equally regardless of race.


What's the "Closest Match" mean at the bottom of the results?

In addition to matching you to the twelve values, the quiz also attempts to match you to a political ideology. This is a work in progress and is much less accurate than the values and axes, so don't take it too seriously. If you disagree with your assigned ideology, sorry!
